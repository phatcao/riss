Welcome to the

Renaissance internation School Saigon

Whether you are an existing or prospective member of our community, or simply interested in learning more about our school, I am sure you will find the various pages and sections of our website informative and helpful. Our school is one of the leading schools in Saigon and has developed an enviable reputation for providing a quality, learning-focused education. We have adopted an International British curriculum tailored to meet the diverse needs of our students in Vietnam. Our academic programme is delivered by highly qualified and enthusiastic teachers. Our curriculum represents the very best of current practice in the United Kingdom, with an international perspective appropriate to our Vietnamese home.


Our students have achieved outstanding end-of-Key Stage, IGCSE and IB Diploma results. Our graduating students enter universities and colleges around the world: principally in the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Singapore, Hong Kong, South Korea, New Zealand and Vietnam.


The school prides itself on being a multi-cultural learning community. Within this community we promote, celebrate and cherish diversity in language and culture; we teach our students to respect and value other peopleâ€™s views, even when different from their own.


Our vibrant co-educational day school has an international enrolment of over 500 students from more than 23 different nationalities, aged from 2 to 18. Our programme of study is delivered in English and is based on the UK National Curriculum (for 2- to 14-year-olds), Cambridge IGCSE (for 14- to 16-year-olds) and the prestigious International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma (for 16- to 18-year-olds). The International Primary Curriculum, an internationally respected learning- focused curriculum found in over 1500 schools worldwide, enhances the Primary School curriculum.
